What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They allow the site and third-party services to recognize your browser, remember preferences, and collect information about how you use the site. Similar technologies — such as pixels and tracking scripts — work in analogous ways and are covered by this policy.

Cookies can be first-party (set by bwcreative.org directly) or third-party (set by external services embedded in the site, such as Google and Meta). They can also be session cookies (deleted when you close your browser) or persistent cookies (stored for a defined period).

Your choices & how to opt out

You have several options for controlling how cookies are used on your device:

  • Browser settings. Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies through their settings. Note that blocking all cookies may affect site functionality. See your browser's help documentation for instructions.
  • Google Analytics opt-out. Install the Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on to prevent GA from collecting data about your visits.
  • Meta advertising preferences. Manage how Meta uses your data for ads at facebook.com/adpreferences or via the Digital Advertising Alliance opt-out at optout.aboutads.info.
  • Do Not Track. Some browsers send a "Do Not Track" signal. This site does not currently respond to DNT signals, as there is no consistent industry standard for doing so.
  • Global Privacy Control (GPC). If your browser broadcasts a GPC signal, we note this as an indication of your privacy preferences. Under CCPA/CPRA, businesses that "sell" or "share" personal data must honor GPC. We do not sell personal data.

Legal basis for processing (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA) or United Kingdom, our legal basis for using non-essential cookies (analytics and marketing) is your consent, as required under the GDPR and ePrivacy Directive. Strictly necessary cookies are processed on the basis of legitimate interest to deliver the service you requested.

You may withdraw your consent at any time by clearing cookies in your browser settings. Note that withdrawing consent does not affect the lawfulness of processing based on consent before its withdrawal.

Analytics and marketing cookies require opt-in consent under GDPR before they are set. If you are located in the EEA and have concerns about our compliance, please contact us.
